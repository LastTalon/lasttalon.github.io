About this website

As mentioned above, this website is for my personal and professional work, mostly containing programs, games, school work, and mods I've made or worked on. It currently doesn't contain much and is in development.

The entire website was designed and created by me to professionally present my work. Its form and function are designed with that in mind, although I am not a web designer. The style is very clean and easy to read, with clear delimiters between sections, hopefully making it easy to follow.

It uses multiple cards on each page, each displaying its own section of information. Each card can have its own information, links, and buttons at the top and bottom. These are the primary content areas of the website.

Any important links can be found on the navigation card. On most desktop browsers the navigation card should follow as you scroll. On small resolution desktop browsers or on most mobile browsers the navigation card will stay at the top of the page, although it can be easily jumped to by clicking the "Top" button at the bottom of each card.

The pages contain both in-text links and buttons at the top and bottom of each card. Both types often link to other sections of information contained on the same page in different cards as well as to other pages. In-text links are used when another section is referenced within the text and buttons are used when its related to the text, but not necessarily mentioned.

The website has both desktop and mobile versions, following the same style with minor changes in each to reflect that particular platform. On a desktop the mobile version can be viewed by making your browser window smaller.
